Arayes

Description

A Middle Eastern food consisting of a flat bread stuffed with raw seasoned meat. It is then fried of grilled until cooked and crispy.

Ingredients

  • 5 Pita Bread
  • Olive Oil Spray

    • Spice Meat Filling

  • 1/2 Brown Onion
  • 500g/1lb Lamb or Beef Mince (Ground meat)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (Finely grated)
  • 1 TBSP Finely Chopped Parsley (Optional)
  • 3 TSP Ground Coriander
  • 3 TSP Ground Cumin
  • 1.5 TSP Smoked Paprika (Or plain Paprika)
  • 0.75 TSP Ground Allspice (Or Mixed Spice)
  • 0.5 TSP Cayenne Pepper (Reduce or omit for less spice)
  • 1.25 TSP cooking/kosher Salt

    • WHIPPED TAHINI YOGURT SAUCE

  • 250g Plain Yogurt
  • 2 TBSP Tahini (or Chinease Sesame Paste)
  • 1 TBSP Lemon Juice
  • 1 Garlic Clove (Finely Grated)
  • 0.5 TSP Cooking/Kosher salt

Instructions

Spice Meat Filling

  1. Grate the onion in a bowl using a standard box grater. We want the juices and all!
  2. Add remaining meat filling ingredients. Mix well with your hands.
  3. Divide stuffing into 10 portiions, flatten into a semi circle shape slightly smaller than the pita bread half.
  4. Gently open the pita then place the meat inside. Close then press to spread to the edge and make it fairly evenly flat.

Cooking Arayes

  1. Preheat the oven to 50C/120F. Place a rack on a tray. This set up is to keep the cooked Arayes warm.
  2. Heat a large frying pan over medium high heat. Spray both sides of the pita with the olive oil spray then place 2 or 3 in the pan. Whatever will fit!
  3. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side, pressing down lightly with a spatula until golden and crispy.
  4. Transfer the cooked Arayes onto the rack and put in the oven to keep warm.

WHIPPED TAHINI SAUCE

  1. Place ingredients into a heatproof bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Microwasve for 15 seconds on high, whisk again and it should resemble soft whipped cream.
